Concerning Frid/a/ys from now on
Fri 14 Sep, 08:28
So after tinkering around a little bit this week I've sadly had to accept the fact that I can no longer do Frid/a/y nights here at R/a/dio. I believe Eku will be taking over as the weekly Frid/a/y DJ. And I think Egg said something about how sundays are shit and he's sticking with tuesdays.

I'm sorry things couldn't work out for me to continue DJing the way I love so very much, but things here are r/a/dio will go on with our weeb music just as they always have.

I'll still be around, and assuming there's still space for me I'll probably DJ again when I come home sometime next year.

These past almost 2 years have been the best I could ever ask for, you guys have been my very best friends I could ever have.

~Kuma

Downtime
Mon 10 Sep, 00:26
Update: We have feature-locked the AFKStreamer and the website. We wont be adding anything new until all known bugs are fixed.

When we say our system is still in development, we mean it; one of the relays went down and the streamer didn't have any handling for this. Whoopsie.

Back up and running with a temporary fix while we commit changes. The stream and website might go down for a bit when we update.

PS: I am working on a fix for the news posts disappearing when the stream goes down. bear with me.

~Hiroto
Site Traffic
Wed 5 Sep, 01:54
I should probably have mentioned this more than 2 months ago, but we actually hit 1 million (confirmed) hits to the site. This number is probably lower than it should be due to adblock (since i know over 80% of you use adblock when i did a little test a few months back involving google ads hidden out of sight), but I'm still projecting hitting 2 million hits by the end of the year, with ~1 million visits.

In addition, a number of you might have noticed that the stream cuts out, or goes into fallback mode from time to time; while we try to keep r/a/dio up 24/7 with no interrupts, we don't have a perfectly stable setup. Our AFK streamer is still in development, after all, and while we do test, it's not until we've been streaming for about 10 hours before something unexpected happens and everything breaks. If you look on the stats page and notice listeners have suddenly dropped to 0, it's usually because the central server restarted.
~Hiroto
